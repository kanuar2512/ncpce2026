Tentang Persidangan About the Conference

"50 Tahun Penguatkuasaan Farmasi: Dari Legasi ke Transformasi Digital, Mendepani Jenayah Farmaseutikal Tanpa Sempadan Demi Kesejahteraan Rakyat" "50 Years of Pharmaceutical Enforcement: From Legacy to Digital Transformation, Confronting Borderless Pharmaceutical Crime for the People's Wellbeing"

🏛️
Penganjur Organiser

Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi (BPF), Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia Pharmaceutical Enforcement Division (BPF), Ministry of Health Malaysia

🎯
Objektif Objective

Menyatukan pegawai penguatkuasaan farmasi bagi berkongsi pengetahuan, pengalaman dan inovasi dalam memerangi jenayah farmaseutikal. To unite pharmaceutical enforcement officers to share knowledge, experience and innovation in combating pharmaceutical crime.

🏅
Jubli Emas Golden Jubilee

Merayakan 50 tahun kecemerlangan penguatkuasaan farmasi Malaysia — satu pencapaian bersejarah yang mencerminkan dedikasi generasi pegawai. Celebrating 50 years of pharmaceutical enforcement excellence in Malaysia — a historic milestone reflecting the dedication of generations of officers.

Sorotan Persidangan Conference Highlights

🏛️
Legasi 50 Tahun A 50-Year Legacy

Meraikan lima dekad penguatkuasaan farmasi Malaysia — daripada pengasasan sehingga era transformasi digital. Celebrate five decades of Malaysian pharmaceutical enforcement — from its founding to the era of digital transformation.

🎤
Pakar Terkemuka Leading Experts

Ceramah pakar daripada PDRM, SKMM, NFCC serta rakan antarabangsa seperti Interpol dan WHO. Expert talks from PDRM, MCMC, NFCC and international partners including Interpol and WHO.

🔬
Inovasi RISE RISE Innovation

Simposium R&D dan inisiatif penambahbaikan kualiti yang memupuk budaya penyelidikan penguatkuasaan. The R&D and quality-improvement symposium fostering a culture of enforcement research.

🌐
Rangkaian & Kerjasama Networking & Collaboration

Jaringan strategik merentas agensi penguatkuasaan, industri dan rakan antarabangsa. Strategic networking across enforcement agencies, industry and international partners.

💡
Transformasi Digital Digital Transformation

Wawasan terkini mendepani jenayah farmaseutikal siber tanpa sempadan melalui teknologi. Cutting-edge insight on confronting borderless cyber pharmaceutical crime through technology.

🏆
Anugerah & Pengiktirafan Awards & Recognition

Meraikan kecemerlangan pegawai dan cawangan melalui anugerah jubli emas yang berprestij. Honouring officer and branch excellence through prestigious golden-jubilee awards.

Pengiktirafan Recognition

Anugerah & Pengiktirafan Awards & Recognition

Logo Jubli Emas 50 Tahun & Lagu Tema Terpilih 50-Year Golden Jubilee Logo & Selected Theme Song

Pelancaran simbolik logo jubli emas 50 tahun penguatkuasaan farmasi bersama persembahan lagu tema persidangan yang baru. Logo ini melambangkan ketegasan dan integriti sepanjang lima dekad penuh cabaran dan transformasi digital. The symbolic launch of the 50-year golden jubilee logo for pharmaceutical enforcement, together with the debut of the new conference theme song. The logo represents steadfastness and integrity across five decades of challenges and digital transformation.

Anugerah Ikon & Penyampaian Medal Icon Award & Medal Presentation

Pengiktirafan tertinggi bagi pegawai yang menunjukkan integriti dan dedikasi luar biasa dalam tugasan penguatkuasaan. Pemenang akan menerima Medal Emas Jubli serta sijil penghargaan khas di hadapan Ketua Pengarah Kesihatan. The highest recognition for officers demonstrating extraordinary integrity and dedication in enforcement duties. Winners receive the Golden Jubilee Medal and a special certificate of appreciation in the presence of the Director-General of Health.

Sijil Penghargaan & Cenderahati Certificates of Appreciation & Souvenirs

Penyampaian sijil penghargaan kepada rakan strategik industri dan agensi penguatkuasaan jemputan yang banyak membantu operasi sepanjang tahun, terutamanya dalam memerangi jenayah farmaseutikal siber. Presentation of certificates of appreciation to strategic industry partners and invited enforcement agencies who have greatly assisted operations throughout the year, particularly in combating cyber pharmaceutical crime.

Anugerah Cawangan CPF Terbaik Best CPF Branch Award

Pengiktirafan kepada cawangan CPF yang menunjukkan prestasi tertinggi dalam operasi penguatkuasaan, integriti, dan inovasi sepanjang tahun. Penilaian dibuat berdasarkan KPI yang ditetapkan oleh BPF. Recognition for the CPF branch demonstrating the highest performance in enforcement operations, integrity, and innovation throughout the year. Assessment is based on KPIs established by BPF.
