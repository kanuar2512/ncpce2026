Tentang Persidangan About the Conference

"50 Tahun Penguatkuasaan Farmasi: Dari Legasi ke Transformasi Digital, Mendepani Jenayah Farmaseutikal Tanpa Sempadan" "50 Years of Pharmaceutical Enforcement: From Legacy to Digital Transformation, Confronting Borderless Pharmaceutical Crime"

Penganjur Organiser

Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi, Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia Pharmacy Enforcement Division, Ministry of Health Malaysia

Objektif Objective

Menjadi platform perkongsian ilmu, hasil Penyelidikan dan Pembangunan (R&D), Inisiatif Penambahbaikan Kualiti serta amalan terbaik bagi memperkukuh penguatkuasaan farmasi melalui kolaborasi strategik dalam menangani jenayah farmaseutikal. To serve as a platform for sharing knowledge, Research and Development (R&D) findings, quality improvement initiatives and best practices — strengthening pharmacy enforcement through strategic collaboration in tackling pharmaceutical crime.

50 Tahun Penguatkuasaan Farmasi 50 Years of Pharmacy Enforcement

Dianjurkan bersempena sambutan 50 Tahun Penguatkuasaan Farmasi bagi memperkukuh ekosistem penguatkuasaan farmasi melalui perkongsian ilmu, Penyelidikan dan Pembangunan (R&D), Inisiatif Penambahbaikan Kualiti serta amalan terbaik, ke arah penguatkuasaan yang lebih berkesan dan progresif. Held in conjunction with the 50th anniversary of Pharmacy Enforcement to strengthen the pharmacy enforcement ecosystem through knowledge sharing, Research and Development (R&D), quality improvement initiatives and best practices — advancing towards more effective and progressive enforcement.

Sorotan Persidangan Conference Highlights

Jubli Emas Penguatkuasaan Farmasi Golden Jubilee of Pharmacy Enforcement

Meraikan 50 tahun kecemerlangan Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi dalam melindungi kesihatan awam dan memperkukuh penguatkuasaan farmaseutikal di Malaysia. Celebrating 50 years of excellence of the Pharmacy Enforcement Division in protecting public health and strengthening pharmaceutical enforcement in Malaysia.

Perkongsian Kepakaran Sharing of Expertise

Menghimpunkan perkongsian ilmu dan pengalaman daripada pakar pelbagai agensi serta warga penguatkuasaan farmasi. Bringing together the sharing of knowledge and experience from experts across agencies and the pharmacy enforcement community.

R&D & Inisiatif Penambahbaikan Kualiti (QIIs) R&D & Quality Improvement Initiatives (QIIs)

Pembentangan hasil penyelidikan dan pembangunan (R&D) serta projek Inisiatif Penambahbaikan Kualiti (QIIs) bagi memacu inovasi dan kecemerlangan penguatkuasaan farmasi. Presenting research and development (R&D) outcomes and Quality Improvement Initiatives (QIIs) projects to drive innovation and excellence in pharmacy enforcement.

Jaringan Strategik Strategic Networking

Memperkukuh jaringan kerjasama antara agensi, industri dan rakan strategik bagi memperkasakan penguatkuasaan farmasi. Strengthening collaborative networks among agencies, industry and strategic partners to empower pharmacy enforcement.

Transformasi Digital Digital Transformation

Meneroka teknologi, inovasi dan pendekatan baharu bagi memperkukuh penguatkuasaan serta mendepani cabaran jenayah farmaseutikal. Exploring technology, innovation and new approaches to strengthen enforcement and confront the challenges of pharmaceutical crime.

Anugerah Jubli Emas Golden Jubilee Awards

Mengiktiraf kecemerlangan pegawai, cawangan dan rakan strategik melalui penyampaian anugerah sempena Jubli Emas Penguatkuasaan Farmasi. Recognising the excellence of officers, branches and strategic partners through award presentations in conjunction with the Golden Jubilee of Pharmacy Enforcement.

Makna di Sebalik Logo Meaning Behind the Logo

Angka 50 The Number 50

Melambangkan kematangan, keteguhan dan kecemerlangan Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi sepanjang lima dekad dalam memartabatkan undang-undang farmasi di Malaysia. Angka ini turut menjadi simbol kredibiliti jentera penguatkuasaan yang kekal relevan merentasi zaman. The number 50 represents the maturity, resilience and excellence the Pharmacy Enforcement Division has built over five decades in upholding pharmacy law in Malaysia. It also stands as a symbol of an enforcement machinery whose credibility has remained relevant across the generations.

Tahun 1976–2026 The Years 1976–2026

Menghayati nostalgia sejarah serta garis masa penubuhan Penguatkuasaan Farmasi bermula tahun 1976 hingga kini. Ia mencerminkan dedikasi berterusan dalam melindungi kesihatan rakyat selama setengah abad. Tracing the historic timeline of Pharmaceutical Enforcement from its establishment in 1976 to the present day, reflecting half a century of unwavering dedication to safeguarding public health.

Ikon Kapsul & Nombor Binari Capsule & Binary Number Icon

Ikon kapsul dan elemen nombor binari melambangkan fokus utama bidang farmaseutikal. Kapsul yang terbelah menggambarkan tindakan proaktif membongkar aktiviti haram serta ketegasan membanteras ubat tidak sah. Elemen nombor binari pula mencerminkan kesiapsiagaan anggota dalam menangani jenayah farmaseutikal dalam talian (e-dagang) serta pemerkasaan teknologi digital dalam penguatkuasaan moden. The capsule icon and binary-number motif represent the core focus of pharmaceutical enforcement. The split capsule signifies proactive action in uncovering illegal activity and firm resolve in combating counterfeit and unregistered medicines, while the binary elements reflect officers' readiness to tackle online (e-commerce) pharmaceutical crime and the growing role of digital technology in modern enforcement.

Elemen Bulatan & Percikan Warna Circle Element & Colour Accents

Bulatan berwarna emas yang mewakili angka "0" melambangkan kesepaduan dan dinamisme dalam mendepani cabaran global. Percikan warna emas, maroon dan kuning menggambarkan keberanian, semangat tinggi dan ketangkasan anggota penguat kuasa dalam memerangi jenayah farmaseutikal secara menyeluruh (360 darjah). The gold circle, forming the number "0", symbolises unity and dynamism in confronting global challenges, while the gold, maroon and yellow accents reflect the courage, spirit and agility of enforcement officers in combating pharmaceutical crime on every front (360 degrees).

Anugerah Ikon Emas & Penyampaian Trofi Golden Icon Award & Trophy Presentation

Pengiktirafan tertinggi kepada pegawai yang menunjukkan integriti dan dedikasi luar biasa serta memberi sumbangan besar dalam aktiviti penguatkuasaan farmasi. Pemenang akan menerima Trofi Jubli Emas serta sijil penghargaan khas, disampaikan oleh Pengarah Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi KKM. The highest recognition awarded to officers who demonstrate outstanding integrity and dedication, and who have made a significant contribution to pharmaceutical enforcement activities. Recipients receive the Golden Jubilee Trophy together with a special certificate of appreciation, presented by the Director of the Pharmacy Enforcement Division, Ministry of Health Malaysia.

Sijil Penghargaan & Cenderahati Certificates of Appreciation & Souvenirs

Penyampaian sijil penghargaan kepada rakan strategik Bahagian Penguatkuasaan Farmasi yang terdiri daripada agensi penguatkuasaan dan institusi dalam negara yang banyak membantu aktiviti penguatkuasaan farmasi, khususnya dalam memerangi jenayah farmaseutikal. Presentation of certificates of appreciation to the Pharmacy Enforcement Division's strategic partners, comprising domestic enforcement agencies and institutions, in recognition of their invaluable support for pharmaceutical enforcement activities, particularly in combating pharmaceutical crime.

Anugerah Kecemerlangan Emas CPF CPF Golden Excellence Award

Pengiktirafan kepada Cawangan Penguatkuasaan Farmasi Negeri yang menunjukkan prestasi keseluruhan cemerlang berdasarkan aspek berikut: Recognition for the State Pharmacy Enforcement Branch that demonstrates outstanding overall performance based on the following aspects:

  • Pencapaian KPI dan outcome penguatkuasaan Achievement of enforcement KPIs and outcomes
  • Kecekapan pengurusan dan tadbir urus Efficiency in management and governance
  • Prestasi perbelanjaan dan pengurusan sumber Financial performance and resource management
  • Inovasi dan penambahbaikan berterusan Innovation and continuous improvement
  • Impak kepada perlindungan kesihatan awam Impact on public health protection
